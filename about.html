About Us

Our Mission

We believe every American deserves financial security and peace of mind. Our mission is to make saving simple, empowering individuals and families to build strong emergency funds that protect their well-being in uncertain times. Whether it’s an unexpected medical bill, job loss, or car repair—having a safety net can change everything. We're here to help make that a reality.

Why We Built This

Too many Americans live paycheck to paycheck, and saving often feels out of reach. We built this platform during a Hackathon to tackle that problem head-on. By making saving intuitive, goal-driven, and accessible, we hope to inspire better habits and long-term security—one emergency fund at a time.

The Team

We're a group of passionate students, designers, and developers who want to make a difference. This is just the beginning—we're learning, building, and growing together.
